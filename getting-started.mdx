## Welcome to Punchey

This short guide helps you get set up quickly, even if you are not tech savvy. You will sign in, explore the dashboard, connect payments, add a few customers or services, and try a test payment.

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  Full step-by-step instructions with screenshots are available in the Punchey Help Center. Visit the Get Started section here: [https://puncheyteam.freshdesk.com/en/support/solutions/158000396728](https://puncheyteam.freshdesk.com/en/support/solutions/158000396728)
</Callout>

## What to do first

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to your account" icon="arrow-right" title-type="p">
    - Open your welcome email and follow the sign-in link.

    - Save your password somewhere safe so you can return anytime.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Look around the dashboard" icon="monitor" title-type="p">
    - Get familiar with the main menu on the left side.

    - You will see tools for payments, customers, reviews, and more.

    - Click a few sections to understand what is available.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect your payment method" icon="credit-card" title-type="p">
    - Go to your payment settings.

    - Follow the prompts to connect your bank for payouts.

    - This allows you to take real customer payments.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add a few customers and services" icon="users" title-type="p">
    - Create a test customer so you can practice.

    - Add the core services or items you sell.

    - Keep it simple; you can add more later.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Run a small test payment" icon="check-circle" title-type="p">
    - Use your test customer and a small amount.

    - This helps you confirm everything works.

    - Do not worry, you can void or refund the test if needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
