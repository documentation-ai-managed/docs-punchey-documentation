## Getting started

Punchey helps small businesses manage payments, engage customers, and maintain stronger relationships through tools that support transactions, communication, and reviews. The platform brings together core business functions so you can streamline operations and focus on delivering great service.

  <Card title="CRM tools" href="crm-tools" icon="users" horizontal="false">
