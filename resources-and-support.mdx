## When to use these resources

- Use the **How-to videos** when you want a quick visual explanation.
- Use the **Knowledge base** when you need clear written steps.
- Check **Product updates** to stay informed about new features.
- Use **App login** to jump straight into your Punchey tools.

<Callout kind="info">

If you are unsure which resource to choose, start with the knowledge base or videos. Both are designed for non-technical business owners and cover the most common tasks in plain language.
