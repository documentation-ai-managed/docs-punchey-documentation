## Customer relationship management

Punchey's CRM tools help you understand customers, streamline data collection, and manage ongoing engagement. You can explore detailed customer profiles, maintain accurate records, and use digital forms to simplify onboarding and service workflows.

A full overview of customer tools and forms is available in the Punchey Resource Center. For step‑by‑step instructions, visit the Customers & Forms section there.

<Columns cols="3">
  <Card title="Customer profiles" href="#profiles" icon="user" horizontal="false">
    Maintain detailed customer profiles with contact information, transaction history, and notes that help you learn more about your customers.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Communication tools" href="#communication" icon="message-square" horizontal="false">
    Reach customers through targeted email or SMS messages when you need to share updates or request information.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Digital forms" href="#forms" icon="file-text" horizontal="false">
    Use online forms to collect customer details and streamline your data‑gathering process.
  </Card>
</Columns>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Import customer data" icon="upload" title-type="p">
    Bring in customer records from spreadsheets or other systems to create a unified profile for each customer.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create useful segments" icon="users" title-type="p">
    Organize customers into groups based on shared attributes, visit frequency, or past transactions.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Use forms to collect information" icon="settings" title-type="p">
    Set up digital forms to gather customer details before appointments or during check‑in.
  </Step>
</Steps>
