## Frequently asked questions

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="How do I sign in to my Punchey account?" default-open="false">

    You can sign in from the main Punchey dashboard using the email and password you registered with. If you need a quick refresher on basics, see [Getting Started](/getting-started) or review password and access options in [Manage Your Account & Billing](/manage-account-billing).

  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Where can I get help if I am stuck?" default-open="false">

    You can browse the full set of support articles at the Punchey Help Center. For a broader set of FAQs, visit the external page at [https://puncheyteam.freshdesk.com/en/support/solutions/158000419313](https://puncheyteam.freshdesk.com/en/support/solutions/158000419313). You can also contact support directly from your dashboard.

  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="How do I take my first payment?" default-open="false">

    From your dashboard, choose the option to start a payment. You can enter the amount, select your customer, and complete the payment using your preferred method. For related concepts, see [Take Payments & Get Paid](/payment-solutions) and the overview in [How Money Moves with Punchey](/money-flow).

  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Can I issue a refund?" default-open="false">

    Yes. Open the payment you want to refund from your history, then choose the refund option. For more context on how payments and credits move through the system, see [Take Payments & Get Paid](/payment-solutions) and optionally [How Money Moves with Punchey](/money-flow).

  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="When will my payouts arrive?" default-open="false">

    Payout timing depends on your account setup and bank processing times. Once a payout is sent, you can view its status from your dashboard under the payouts section. For a deeper look at payout flow and reconciliations, see [How Money Moves with Punchey](/money-flow) and related summaries in [Reports](/reports).

  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="How do I update my account details?" default-open="false">

    In your profile or settings area, you can update business information, contact details, and billing information. For more detail on account-level controls, see [Manage Your Account & Billing](/manage-account-billing) and team-specific settings in [Team & Locations](/team-and-locations).

  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Where can I learn more?" default-open="false">

    For a full library of help topics, visit the Punchey Help Center at [https://puncheyteam.freshdesk.com/en/support/solutions](https://puncheyteam.freshdesk.com/en/support/solutions). You can also explore the broader platform in the [Introduction](/introduction) and find additional guidance under [Resources & Support](/resources-and-support).

  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>
