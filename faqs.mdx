## Frequently asked questions

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="How do I sign in to my Punchey account?" default-open="false">
    You can sign in from the main Punchey dashboard using the email and password you registered with. If you forget your password, select the reset option on the sign-in screen to receive a reset link.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Where can I get help if I am stuck?" default-open="false">
    You can browse the full set of support articles at the Punchey Help Center. For a broader set of FAQs, visit the external page at [https://puncheyteam.freshdesk.com/en/support/solutions/158000419313](https://puncheyteam.freshdesk.com/en/support/solutions/158000419313). You can also contact support directly from your dashboard.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="How do I take my first payment?" default-open="false">
    From your dashboard, choose the option to start a payment. You can enter the amount, select your customer, and complete the payment using your preferred method. The steps guide you through the full process on screen.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Can I issue a refund?" default-open="false">
    Yes. Open the payment you want to refund from your history, then choose the refund option. You can refund the full amount or select a partial amount.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="When will my payouts arrive?" default-open="false">
    Payout timing depends on your account setup and bank processing times. Once a payout is sent, you can view its status from your dashboard under the payouts section.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="How do I update my account details?" default-open="false">
    In your profile or settings area, you can update business information, contact details, and billing information. Changes take effect immediately once saved.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Where can I learn more?" default-open="false">
    For a full library of help topics, visit the Punchey Help Center at [https://puncheyteam.freshdesk.com/en/support/solutions](https://puncheyteam.freshdesk.com/en/support/solutions).
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>
