## Frequently asked questions

<ExpandableGroup>

  <Expandable title="How do I sign in to my Punchey account?">

You can sign in from the main Punchey dashboard using the email and password you registered with. If you forget your password, select the reset option on the signâ€‘in screen to receive a reset link.

  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Where can I get help if I am stuck?">

You can browse the full set of support articles at the Punchey Resource Center. For a broader set of FAQs, visit the external page at https://support.punchey.com/faqs. You can also contact support directly from your dashboard.

  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="How do I take my first payment?">

From your dashboard, choose the option to start a payment. You can enter the amount, select your customer, and complete the payment using your preferred method. The steps guide you through the full process on screen.

  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Can I issue a refund?">

Yes. Open the payment you want to refund from your history, then choose the refund option. You can refund the full amount or select a partial amount.

  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="When will my payouts arrive?">

Payout timing depends on your account setup and bank processing times. Once a payout is sent, you can view its status from your dashboard under the payouts section.

  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="How do I update my account details?">

In your profile or settings area, you can update business information, contact details, and billing information. Changes take effect immediately once saved.

  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Where can I learn more?">

For a full library of help topics, visit the Punchey Resource Center at https://support.punchey.com/.

  </Expandable>

</ExpandableGroup>
