## Online Reputation Management

Managing online reviews is crucial for small businesses. Punchey's review management tools help you monitor, respond to, and leverage customer feedback to improve your reputation and attract new customers.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Platforms" icon="link">
    Link your Google My Business, Yelp, and other review platforms to centralize feedback.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor Reviews" icon="eye">
    Set up automated alerts for new reviews and track sentiment across platforms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Respond Strategically" icon="message-square">
    Use our response templates and AI suggestions to craft professional replies.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<CodeGroup tabs="REST API,Webhook">
  <Tab title="REST API" icon="code">
    ````javascript
    // Fetch recent reviews
    const reviews = await fetch('/api/reviews?platform=google&limit=10', {
      headers: { 'Authorization': `Bearer ${apiKey}` }
    }).then(r => r.json());
    
    // Respond to a review
    await fetch(`/api/reviews/${reviewId}/response`, {
      method: 'POST',
      headers: {
        'Authorization': `Bearer ${apiKey}`,
        'Content-Type': 'application/json'
      },
      body: JSON.stringify({ response: 'Thank you for your feedback!' })
    });
    ````
  </Tab>
