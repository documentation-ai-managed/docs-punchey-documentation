## Using review insights to improve your business

Review feedback offers a clear view of what customers appreciate and where you can improve. By monitoring recurring themes and sentiment trends, you can identify training opportunities, refine service offerings, and highlight standout team performance. Use your strongest reviews in marketing materials or on-site displays to build trust with potential customers.

<Callout kind="success" collapsed="false">
  Many businesses see improved retention when they consistently act on customer feedback and showcase positive experiences.
</Callout>
